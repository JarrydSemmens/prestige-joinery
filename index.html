Quality you can feel

Prestige Joinery is a small, local workshop in Carrum Downs, Victoria. We work with carefully selected Australian hardwoods — blackwood, jarrah, Victorian ash and more — and finish every piece by hand.

No flat-pack, no shortcuts, no mass production. Just honest joinery, precise detail and furniture made to be passed down.

How It Works

From first conversation to final delivery, commissioning a piece is simple and collaborative.

Consult

Tell us what you're after. We discuss size, timber, finish and budget — no obligation.

Design

We prepare a design and fixed quote so you know exactly what you're getting.

Craft

Your piece is built by hand in our workshop, with progress photos along the way.

Deliver

Pick up from Carrum Downs, or we deliver across Melbourne's south-east.
